About Claudia:

Claudia is a student at the University of Washington, majoring in Computer Science and minoring in ELS (Education, Learning and Society).

In her free time, she loves to dance, sing along to throwbacks, and spend time with family.

Growing up, Claudia wanted to be a star. She loved to act, sing and perform. Once she began High School, she found a new passion in leadership. She excelled in Student Government, becoming Vice President during her senior year, and was a Varsity Cheerleader. She was also on the Varsity Golf Team. While she still loves the performing arts, she is now invested in learning about technology.
